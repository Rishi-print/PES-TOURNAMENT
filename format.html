  1. The tournament requires a minimum of 4 players. The total number of participants may vary.
  2. After the round-robin group stage, the top 4 players will advance to the semi-finals. This will be followed by a Final and a Third Place Playoff.
  3. Group Stage Scoring:
    - Win: 3 points
    - Draw: 1 point
    - Loss: 0 points
  4. If teams are tied on points, the ranking will be decided by Goal Difference (GD). The player with the superior GD will secure the higher position.
