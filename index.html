⚖️ Tournament Rules

  1. A player cannot play back passes with the goalkeeper inside the penalty box more than three consecutive times during a game.
  2. In case of lag, players must record at least a 10-second video as proof to establish the issue.
  3. All pending matches must be completed by Sunday of the ongoing week. If not, the match will be awarded as a win to the player willing to play.
  4. Tournament records will be maintained on the website by admins selected via nomination.
